          Shangri La

          Tour Itinerary

          ITINERARY 10 days / 9 nights

          1

          day

          Beijing

          Arrival in Beijing, capital of the PRC. Transfer to the Hotel and accommodation.

          2

          day

          Beijing

          Buffet Breakfast+Lunch+Dinner Full-day Tour including the Great Wall and Summer Palace. During the tour we invite customers to visit the cloiseonné workshop. and then we will enjoy a typical dinner in which we will taste the “ Delicious Beijing Roasted Duck". Visit to the Bird's Nest and the Olympic swimming center known as the "Water Cube" (no entrance). Acrobatics Show. Accommodation at the Hotel.

          3

          day

          Beijing

          Breakfast buffet + lunch That day we will make the visit of the city including Tian An men Square, the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven and the Silk Market. Hotel accommodation.

          4

          day

          Beijing - Lijiang

          Lijiang Buffet breakfast At the indicated time transfer to the airport to depart in domestic flight to Lijiang. In the afternoon we will visit, the historic center of the city declared by Unesco as a World Heritage Site, Accommodation in the Hotel.

          5

          day

          Lijiang

          Buffet Breakfast + Lunch Bus transfer to Jade Dragon Mountain, at whose summit of 4,500 meters we will ascend by funicular. On the way we will visit the village of Baisha, at the foot of the snowy jade dragon mountain. Transfer to the Hotel and accommodation

          6

          day

          Lijiang - Shangri-La (car)

          Buffet Breakfast + Lunch Trip to Zhongdian (Shangri-La) down the incredible Yangtze River Gorge where we will visit the famous Tiger Leap. We continue to ascend to the population of Shangri-La, capital of The Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Deqin. Arrival and accommodation.

          7

          day

          Shangri-La

          Breakfast buffet + Lunch Visit to Songzanglin Monastery, also known as the "Little Potala". Next we will head towards the old city but first we will pass by Lake Napa where we can enjoy a wonderful landscape in addition to being able to admire the local fauna: yaks, wild horses, wild ducks, etc. Accommodation.

          8

          day

          Shangri - La-Shanghai (plane)

          Breakfast buffet In the morning flight to Shanghai via Kunming. Arrival and Transfer to the Hotel. Accommodation at the Hotel.

          9

          day

          Shanghai

          Shanghai Buffet Breakfast + Lunch City Tour with Jade Buddha Temple, Yuyuan Garden, Malecon and Nanjing Street, Afternoon free time.

          10

          day

          Departure

          Buffet breakfast. At the agreed time transfer Hotel-Apt. END OF OUR SERVICES.
