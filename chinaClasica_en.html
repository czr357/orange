          Classic China

          Tour Itinerary

          ITINERARY 10 days / 9 nights

          1

          day

          Beijing

          Arrival in Beijing, capital of the PRC. Transfer to the Hotel and accommodation.

          2

          day

          Beijing

          Buffet Breakfast+Lunch+Dinner Full-day Tour including the Great Wall and Summer Palace. During the tour we invite customers to visit the cloiseonné workshop. and then we will enjoy a typical dinner in which we will taste the “ Delicious Beijing Roasted Duck". Visit to the Bird's Nest and the Olympic swimming center known as the "Water Cube" (no entrance). Acrobatics Show. Accommodation at the Hotel.

          3

          day

          Beijing

          Breakfast buffet + lunch That day we will make the visit of the city including Tian An men Square, the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven and the Silk Market. Hotel accommodation.

          4

          day

          Beijing-Xian (Train)

          Buffet breakfast At the indicated time transfer to the railway station to take the High Speed Train to Xian, starting point of the Millennium "Silk Road". Arrival. Transfer to the Hotel and accommodation in the Hotel.

          5

          day

          Xian

          Buffet Breakfast+Lunch. During your stay in Xian we will visit the Museum of Warriors and Terracotta Horses of Qin Shi Huang Mausoleum, the pagoda of the small wild forest and the ancient wall of the city. Accommodation at the Hotel.

          6

          day

          Xian - Shanghai (plane)

          Breakfast buffet In the morning flight to Shanghai, the great shopping, industrial and cultural center of China. Arrival. Accommodation at the Hotel.

          7

          day

          Shanghai

          Buffet Breakfast City tour with Jade Buddha Temple, Yuyuan Garden, Malecon and Nanjing Street, Afternoon free time.

          8

          day

          Shanghai – Guilin(plane)

          Buffet breakfast At the indicated time transfer to the airport to depart by domestic flight to Guilin, located by the Lijiang River. Arrival, transfer to the Hotel and accommodation.

          9

          day

          Guilin - Yangsuo - Guangzhou (Train)

          Buffet Breakfast+Lunch aboard the Li River Cruise Cruise. Until you reach Yangshuo village, enjoying the pedestrian street. Visit to the Cane Flute Cave. Transfer to the railway station to take the high-speed train to Guilin. Arrival and Accommodation in the Hotel.

          10

          day

          Guangzhou – Hong Kong

          Buffet Breakfast + Lunch In the morning we will visit the Chen Family's Ancestral House, Huacheng Square, where you can watch TV Tower (without climbing), and Qingping Market. In the afternoon transfer to the pier "LianHua Shan" to take the Ferry to Hong Kong. End of our services.
